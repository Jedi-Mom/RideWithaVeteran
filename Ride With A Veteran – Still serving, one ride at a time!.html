Uber or Lyft Driver

While you can’t specifically ask for me via Uber or lyft, you can reach me via my Facebook page, Ride With a Veteran, call or text and I can be in your area.

Schedule a ride in advance

Schedule a ride in advance or even a series of rides. We’ll happily put you on our calendar and make sure that we’re in your area when you need us.

 

Why contact us?

Why contact us when there’s so many other drivers out there? Well we’re dependable, honest and trustworthy. We’re not saying the other drivers aren’t, but we’re putting that out there in writing so you can see it.

We’re a family owned company, contracted to Uber and Lyft. We have a Chrysler Town and Country, so we’re big enough for small groups to ride together. With heated seats in the winter (front passenger and the two captain chairs in the second row), we can get you where you need to go warmly in the winter. We even have a DVD player that should you find yourself with a child needing entertainment during the ride, we’ll happily pop in a video to keep them calm for the duration.

Have some luggage, we’ve got room for that too!

New in the area and need advice on where to eat, what to do…we can help you find the right spot for your needs.
