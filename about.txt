            		<h1 style="text-align: center;">Hi, I’m Mike&#8230;a Veteran</h1>
<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">
<p>Hi and welcome to my page!  I’m Mike and I decided to become a driver for Uber and lyft so that I could get out there and meet the very people that I served and protected during my service in the Army.</p>

<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">
<h2>A little about me&#8230;</h2>
</div>
<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">Well, where to begin. I’m just your average guy who has a family like everyone else. I served in the Army during the tragedy of 9/11. I have been overseas and in war zones. I have seen the bad, the very bad and the stuff you just can’t talk about.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">But I’ve also seen the good and the great. I’ve helped the less fortunate and I’ve made my country a safer place.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">After coming back to the states and putting away my uniform for good, I just couldn’t find my niche in the civilian life&#8230;.until now.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="_2cuy _3dgx _2vxa">I love driving around town, interacting with the people who have hired me to get them from point A to point B. I love hearing their stories, putting faces to the crowd who cheered my brethren and me from afar. It’s a wonderful feeling being able to once again help in some capacity the masses I once served.</div>
<h1 style="text-align: center;">Meet the Crew</h1>
<p style="text-align: center;">Here we are&#8230;the Ride with a Veteran team.  We&#8217;re a small, family owned business right now.   Currently we have one driver and two behind the scenes jack of all trades.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><em>Founder &amp; Driver</em></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">You&#8217;ll love riding with Mike.  He&#8217;s an easy going geek who can tell you quite a few of the hotspots to go in Louisville and Southern Indiana.  He&#8217;s a local boy to area even though he spent a few years serving in the Army and moving to different bases throughout the US.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">He can tell you stories about far off places or let you in on the secrets of getting around during rush hour.  You choose the topic and he&#8217;ll keep you entertained until you safely arrive at your destination.</p>
